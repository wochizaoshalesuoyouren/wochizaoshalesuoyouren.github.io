Are automated vehicles good for the people, the environment and the future？

 Now more and more people are optimistic about automotive vehicles. What is the reason? It is most likely that automated vehicles are good for the people, the environment and the future. First is can make people safer. According to the assay written by Edward Humes named the promise of the post-driver life that in 2015, the number of road deaths in the United States is 38300,4.4 million seriously injured.People may cause a lot of trouble when they are driving, which may hurt themselves and others. This can be avoided with the autonomous vehicle, because the autonomous vehicle will not be as careless as people and will not do things emotionally. So it is good for people. 

 The other is it has improved traffic circulation and reduced pollution. Since automotive vehicles can drive safely in narrower lanes and better plan routes, the traffic will be more flowing in future.In fact,autonomous vehicles are electric vehicles. They do not need gasoline and will not produce pollutants. In the future, they will have better air quality environment.

 Let's go back to the security.Some people claim that level 3 autonomous vehicle that is also need human involve, it still needs human beings,which is also prone to problems. The sudden need of mankind makes accidents unavoidable. Isn't it more unsafe? But in fact, the famous company Google Waymo already can produces Level 5 – the fully autonomous car. In this way, the problem of "also need human to work" can be solved.Then human don't need to worry about security. Autonomous vehicles enable people to drive more safely, solve the problem of traffic congestion, and reduce pollution. From these, we can see that automated vehicles are good for the people, the environment and the future.
